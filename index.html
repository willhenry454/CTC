What is the CTC?

The Computer Technology Club is a student-run organization that functions under the guidance of our academic advisor: Dr. Paul Mullins. The Executive Board of the CTC comprises of a President, Vice President, Secretary, Treasurer, and Public Relation officer who are elected annually in April.
